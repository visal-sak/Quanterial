A quick reference for the terms used throughout these docs.

## Content terms

**Question** — the smallest unit of practice: a single check with an answer and
feedback.

**Lesson** — one focused idea, explained briefly and reinforced with practice.
Usually 5–10 minutes. See [Core Concepts](/docs/core-concepts).

**Course** — an ordered collection of lessons around a single topic.

**Learning path** — a curated sequence of courses toward a goal. See
[Learning Paths](/docs/learning-paths).

**Capstone** — a larger project at the end of a path that pulls everything together.

## Progress terms

**Progress** — how much content you've *completed* (lessons, courses, paths).

**Mastery** — a 0–1 score estimating how well you *know* a topic right now. See
[Mastery & Progress](/docs/mastery).

**Decay** — the gradual drop in mastery over time that powers spaced review.

**Streak** — the number of consecutive days you've learned something.

**Review queue** — the daily list of topics whose mastery has dipped, ready for a
quick refresh.

## Practice terms

**Active recall** — retrieving information from memory instead of re-reading it.

**Spaced repetition** — reviewing at increasing intervals for efficient retention.

**Hint** — a nudge toward the next step that stops short of revealing the answer.

**Placement quiz** — a short assessment that measures existing mastery so paths can
skip what you know.

## Account terms

**Dashboard** — your home base showing progress, streaks, and your review queue.

**Certificate** — a shareable proof of completing a learning path.
