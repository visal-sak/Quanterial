Quick answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? See
[Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting).

## Getting started

### Is Quantum Learning free?

You can start learning for free. Some advanced courses and learning paths are part
of a paid plan — you'll always see clearly which content requires an upgrade.

### Do I need any prior experience?

No. Every learning path has a beginner-friendly entry point, and the placement quiz
routes you to the right starting spot. Start with
[Beginner Paths](/docs/learning-paths/beginner).

### Do I need to install anything?

No. Everything runs in your browser on any device.

## Learning & lessons

### How long are the lessons?

Most lessons take **5–10 minutes**. They're intentionally short so you can make
progress even on a busy day.

### What if I get a question wrong?

That's great, actually — corrected mistakes are the strongest learning signal.
You'll get an explanation and the topic will come back in your review queue. See
[Practice & Feedback](/docs/practice).

### Can I skip things I already know?

Yes. Take the placement quiz and [learning paths](/docs/learning-paths) will trim
topics you've already mastered.

## Progress & accounts

### How is my progress saved?

Progress saves automatically after every lesson and practice attempt. You can pick
up exactly where you left off from any device.

### Can I learn on mobile?

Yes. The platform is fully responsive and works in any modern browser on phone,
tablet, or desktop. Your progress syncs automatically across devices.

### What is a streak?

A streak counts the consecutive days you've learned something. It's a simple, powerful
way to build a daily habit.

### What are mastery and the review queue?

**Mastery** measures how well you know each topic; the **review queue** resurfaces
topics before you forget them. Full details on
[Mastery & Progress](/docs/mastery).

## Billing

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Paid plans can be cancelled at any time, and you keep access until the end of the
billing period.
