Welcome to **Quantum Learning**, a modern learning platform built to help you go
from curious to confident. These docs walk you through everything you need to start
learning, track your progress, and get the most out of the platform.

## What is Quantum Learning?

Quantum Learning organizes knowledge into small, focused **lessons** grouped into
**courses**, and sequences those courses into guided **learning paths**. Every lesson
pairs a short explanation with hands-on practice, so you learn by doing instead of
just reading.

The platform then tracks your **mastery** of each topic and uses it to recommend the
single best thing for you to study next — and to remind you to review before you
forget.

## How it fits together

Small pieces roll up into big outcomes:

**Question → Lesson → Course → Learning Path → Your Goal**

Questions build lessons, lessons build courses, and courses build the path to your
goal — each layer sequenced and paced for you.
