This guide gets you from zero to your first completed lesson in a few minutes.

## Before you begin

You'll need:

- A device with a modern browser (phone, tablet, or computer).
- An email address, or a Google/GitHub account for single sign-on.
- About 10 minutes.

That's it — there's nothing to install.

## 1. Create your account

Sign up with an email address or a supported single sign-on provider. Once you're
in, you'll land on your **dashboard** — your home base for everything you're learning.

<Callout title="Verify your email">
  If you sign up with email, click the verification link so we can save your progress
  and send review reminders.
</Callout>

## 2. Take the placement quiz (optional but recommended)

The short placement quiz measures what you already know so paths can skip it. It takes
about 3 minutes and can save you hours. You can always retake or skip it later.

## 3. Choose a course or path

Browse the catalog and pick a course that matches your goal, or start a guided
[learning path](/docs/learning-paths) if you want the ordering handled for you.

  <Card title="Browse Learning Paths" href="/docs/learning-paths" />
  <Card title="Understand Core Concepts" href="/docs/core-concepts" />

## 4. Complete your first lesson

Each lesson follows the same simple loop:

- **Learn** — read a short, focused explanation.
- **Practice** — apply it immediately with a hands-on exercise.
- **Feedback** — get instant, explanatory feedback.
- **Review** — a quick recap of what you learned.

```ts
// Mark a lesson complete via the API
await quantum.progress.complete({
  courseId: 'intro-to-quantum',
  lessonId: 'welcome',
});
```

## 5. Keep your streak

Come back daily to build a streak. Streaks are the simplest way to turn learning
into a habit — even 10 minutes a day adds up fast.

<Callout title="Tip">
  Turn on email or push reminders from **Settings → Notifications** so you never
  break a streak by accident.
</Callout>

## 6. Clear your daily review

Once you've learned a few things, your dashboard shows a **review queue** — a handful
of questions on topics that are starting to fade. Clearing it takes a few minutes and
is the highest-leverage habit on the platform. Learn why on
[Mastery & Progress](/docs/mastery).

## Troubleshooting

| Problem                        | Fix                                             |
| ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Didn't get the verification email | Check spam, then resend from the login screen |
| Progress didn't save            | Make sure you're signed in on this device       |
| A lesson won't load             | Refresh the page; see [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting) |
