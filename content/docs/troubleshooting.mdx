Most issues have a quick fix. Work through the relevant section below; if nothing
helps, contact support with the details listed at the bottom.

## Account & sign-in

### I didn't get the verification email

1. Check your spam or promotions folder.
2. Make sure you typed your address correctly.
3. Use **Resend verification** on the login screen.
4. Add our domain to your contacts and try again.

### I can't sign in

- Confirm you're using the same method you signed up with (email vs. Google/GitHub).
- Reset your password from the login screen.
- Clear cookies for the site, then retry.

## Progress & syncing

### My progress didn't save

- Make sure you were **signed in** on the device you used.
- Progress syncs when you're online — if you were offline, reconnect and reopen the
  lesson.
- Give it a moment; sync can lag a few seconds on slow connections.

### My progress is different on another device

Sign out and back in on both devices to force a fresh sync. Progress is tied to your
account, not the device.

## Content & lessons

### A lesson won't load

1. Refresh the page (**Ctrl+Shift+R** / **Cmd+Shift+R** for a hard refresh).
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Disable extensions that block scripts, then retry.

### A page shows "Not Found"

The link may be outdated or the page moved. Use the sidebar or search to find the
current page.

## Performance

### The site feels slow

- Close unused tabs to free memory.
- Try a different network.
- Update your browser to the latest version.

## Still stuck?

Contact support and include:

| Detail            | Why it helps                       |
| ----------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| What you did      | Reproduce the issue                |
| What you expected | Understand the goal                |
| What happened     | See the actual behavior            |
| Device & browser  | Narrow down environment problems   |
| A screenshot      | A picture is worth a thousand words |
