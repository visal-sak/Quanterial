Plenty of platforms hand you videos and hope for the best. Quantum Learning is built
around a few evidence-backed ideas about how people actually learn — and everything
in the product follows from them.

## The problems we set out to fix

- **Passive learning doesn't stick.** Watching and re-reading feel productive but
  fade fast.
- **One-size-fits-all wastes time.** You either sit through things you know or get
  lost in things you don't.
- **Cramming beats spacing — until the exam.** Then it's gone.
- **No feedback loop.** Without knowing *why* you were wrong, mistakes repeat.

## The principles we build on

### 1. Active recall

Retrieving information strengthens memory far more than reviewing it. So every lesson
makes you *produce* answers, not just recognize them. See
[Practice & Feedback](/docs/practice).

### 2. Spaced repetition

Reviewing at increasing intervals — right before you'd forget — is dramatically more
efficient than massed practice. Our [mastery](/docs/mastery) model schedules this for
you automatically.

### 3. Adaptivity

Time is your scarcest resource. [Learning paths](/docs/learning-paths) skip what you
already know and focus on what you don't.

### 4. Immediate, explanatory feedback

Feedback that arrives instantly and explains the *reasoning* turns every mistake into
a lesson.

## How the principles show up in the product

| Principle          | Where you see it                                  |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Active recall      | Practice after every concept                       |
| Spaced repetition  | The daily review queue                             |
| Adaptivity         | Placement quiz + path trimming                     |
| Explanatory feedback | The "why" shown after each answer                |

## Who it's for

Quantum Learning works for self-taught beginners, students, career-switchers, and
professionals brushing up. If you want to *actually retain* what you study — not just
watch it go by — you're in the right place.
